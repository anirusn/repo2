A little something about us



Founded in 1992 in Pokhara, Nepal, Arjun Furnitures today is one of the most respected and admired home furnishings companies in the Nepal. Our dining, bedroom, home entertainment, home office and accent furniture is distinguished by beautiful quality craftsmanship and functional features to make your life easier. During over 25 years of operation, our priority has always been to maintain strong relationships with employees, customers, sales representatives and suppliers. Inspired by the company's core values - Integrity, Caring, Listening, Innovation, Service, Responsibility, Citizenship and Honesty - Hooker Furniture gives back to the communities in which we live and work through foundations, active community service and socially and environmentally responsible initiatives.


Our History and achievements

1992 Sep. 2

The first commercial business was started in the name Arjun Furnitures, initially a wood reseller in pokhara. The name derives from the name of founder Arjun Neupane.

2000 Sept. 5

The business starts designing its own furniture. Over time, Arjun Furnitures becomes a concept furniture for every home.

2007 Mar. 5

Second store outside Pokhara is established in kathmandu, followed in the coming years by stores in Butwal and Chitwan.

2015 July 9

Arjun Furnitures becomes the second multi-millionare company in Nepal.

2018 July 31

To improve the Arjun Furnitures franchise system and clarify roles,its range, supply and production activities were transferred to the new Inter furnitures Group headed by AN Group of companies The ownership changes came into effect on 31 July 2018.
